About Short Opportunities

Don't spend hours searching for something to do. Find the right opportunities here.

Due to COVID-19, many recent graduates and students are looking for short-term opportunites especially in Fall 2020 as schools move online and jobs are postponed. While other job sites exist, they are hard to navigate for only short-term positions and do not offer other opportunites such as volunteering, non-profit, and teaching opportunites.

This is the sentiment I've heard from my close friends, classmates, Linkedin feed, and Slack channels. Currently, several companies have postponed their start dates including: Linkedin BLP,Deloitte Consulting,Oliver Wyman, McKinsey & Company,Bain & Company, Boston Consulting Group, and Credera. These positions now start late Fall 2020 or early Spring 2021. Yet, the impact is so much larger as many others face delays or unemployment.

Short Opportunities is for you. For the recent graduate, whose full-time start date is delayed and and needs to fund their next step in life. For the recent graduate, whose full-time offer got cancelled. Now is the chance, to pivot into your dream role. For the current university student, who is feeling uneasy about another semester on Zoom or cannot attend in-person classes.

For the non-profit, looking to find someone who is passionate about your cause. For the startup, looking to hire someone in the fall. For the organization, looking for someone to teach workshops weekly. For the university, looking for someone to help with reasearch. And for many other possibilites that we are unaware of. Short Opportunities is for all of you.
Now for some details. 1. We don't scrape data. Everything is submitted by a real person. This helps us know that our job postings are active and real. 2. We don't receive any percentage of commission. We just want to help you find something to do :)

Happy searching! If you have any feedback, please drop a line at shortopportunities@gmail.com or join join our Facebook Group to connect.
